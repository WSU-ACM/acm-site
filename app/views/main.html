Our Mission

Prepare graduates for an exciting and challenging career in technology.

We are a group of students at Washington State University working hard to make sure that our students are ready for diverse challenges. Some of the ways that we prepare students are by: offering extra student-led tutoring sessions, hosting technology workshops, supporting smaller clubs and hosing an annual hackathon.
